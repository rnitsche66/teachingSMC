Goal and contribution

The paper proposes a controller for quadrotor unmanned aerial vehicles
(QUAVs) that guarantees fixed-time convergence in the presence of both
matched and mismatched disturbances.  The proposed method is
singularity-free (in particular by using quaternions for attitude
representation) and avoids discontinuities at the origin, yielding a
bounded and smooth control output.  The effectiveness of the approach
is validated through numerical simulations and experiments on a
low-cost quadrotor platform.

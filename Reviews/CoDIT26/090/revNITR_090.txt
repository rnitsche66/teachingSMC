I know that "C-u 80 C-x f" sets the fill width 80, and M-q
adjusts it. How to make it default? I mean, how to make
width 80 deafault for fill width?

In your .emacs place (setq-default fill-column 80) Or you
can just use M-x customize-variable on fill-column, and it
will set it and save it for you using the standard customize
interface

fill-column is a buffer-local variable, which is why you
need setq-default, not just setq.

taken from:
https://stackoverflow.com/questions/3566727/how-to-set-the-default-width-of-fill-mode-to-80-with-emacs

On the fixed-time control of mismatched perturbations: An application to
Quadrotor Unmanned Aerial Vehicles

#_090

Authors: Artega et al
Mexico and France


Short summary:
+++++++++++++

The paper introduces a sliding-mode–based fixed-time
control framework for QUAVs that provides robust
fixed-time convergence properties against both matched and
mismatched disturbances.  The approach is practical
(singularity-free, smooth control signals without hard
switching) and is demonstrated convincingly in
simulations.  The concept is suitable for demanding
robotics and industrial applications that require fast,
guaranteed convergence times.
